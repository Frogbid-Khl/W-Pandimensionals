Edith Granville-Smith

Fourth Bureau Addendum

*******************************************

Edith Granville-Smith was raised on a series of army bases around the globe; she learnt to speak English in South London. After her mother’s disappearance, the 14th Duchess of Broadcliffs, Edith spent the following years in various international schools before returning to the ancestral seat, Granville Manor.

A model student, but previous records indicate a history of violence. Although she is active in many school and extracurricular activities, she has no known associates.

She is however a suspected associate of Marlowe Jones and Tiberius Soames.
