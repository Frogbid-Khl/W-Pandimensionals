Encryption code AZ+3

REF (23/22) Division 66bd/Subsection Orientation Basic Primary Clause: Vehicles and Weapons

*******************************************

Much has already been written, conjectured, and theorised about the exact nature and origin of the rightly feared Void Ship. Only the most advanced species can even detect the quantum fractures the ship leave in its wake.

And even these Pandimensional civilisations have trouble stopping them. 

A single ship can wipe out a fleet sent against it. On the rare occasions a Void Ship has been bested, they have the unfortunate habit of imploding into a black hole, thus making any encounter with them a suicide mission.
